"Zero Day" - Cyberterrorism Novel by Mark Russinovich

2011-01-25

It seems that the respected Windows security and technical guru Mark Russinovich is working on a sci-fi novel related to cybercrime and cyberterrorism called Zero Day, that is set to roll out in March. A pretty uncommon approach, for technically gifted gurus like Mark to turn to novel writing, though i must admit it adds to the weight of book, at least in my opinion.

In the end, this sounds really interesting and worth giving a heads up once this book becomes available.
